Are you looking at buying a house in New Jersey ?

Here is a look into historical home prices across New Jersey counties.., Sep 20, 2018

When you start researching to buy a house, you immediately start looking at sites like Zillow or Trulia. While they provide a lot of information about individual house and its features, it is hard to find high level information like
- what was the average price of homes sold in a township over the years
- how may houses were sold in a year or a month in a particular townhship.

This kind of information will provide some insight into stability of price, strength of housing market, affordability etc

Based on data available in NJ State Department of Treasurey, I have compiled average sale price of home in each township, the number of houses sold in a year and month.
